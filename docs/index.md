## How to Use This Wiki

This wiki is organized into six major sections:

| Section | What It Covers |
|---------|---------------|
| **Core Definitions** | Front-loaded glossary — read this first so no term is encountered without context |
| **History** | Chronological narrative from creation through the present era |
| **Beings** | Individual entries for every named entity, race, and group |
| **Places** | Locations within Deepmere and their significance |
| **Concepts & Forces** | TrueLight, TrueDark, Umbral Essence, and the metaphysical systems |
| **Society** | The Orders, Sparks, Temple of Knowledge, and social structures of Farshadow |

Terms that appear **bolded** throughout this wiki are defined in their own entries. Use the search bar or sidebar to jump to any definition.

## Quick Reference: Timeline

| Period | Key Events |
|--------|-----------|
| **Before the Fall** | Eyru creates Lightmere and the Highborn. Nova conducts secret works in darkness. |
| **The Fall** | Eyru returns to find corruption. Lightmere is lost. |
| **The Scorching** | Eyru unleashes TrueLight. Highborn burned to spirit or diminished. World becomes Deepmere. |
| **Post-Scorching** | Umbra resides within Deepmere. Shades walk the surface. Keepers endure in isolation. |
| **Creation of Kinsfolk** | Eyru sings the Kinsfolk into existence — a separate act, not survivors of the Fall. |
| **Days of Walked Nights** | Kinsfolk depart Eyru, wander through Deepmere, encounter nine Keepers. |
| **The Crossing** | Kinsfolk discover the intersection of Worldlines and found Farshadow. |
| **~10 AF** | The Night-Sun Event — first Archfiend rising at Farshadow, repelled by fire. |
| **~150 AF** | Present day. Next Interval expected. Vigilance has declined. |
